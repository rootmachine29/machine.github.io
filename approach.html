Work

Our Process

01. First meeting

As soon as you get in touch we will
reach you and arrange a meeting.
We will listen your business
challenges as well as what you
expect from Develpify.

02. Project
Defination

This is the stage where we define
the project needs and create a
refined budget efficient operation
list for the project, estimated
delivery time and the budget.

03. Start off

if we agree on all the terms Develpify is
ready to start working for you. Next
step is signing the contract handling
the initial payments and starting the
project

04. Discovery

At this step we define the project
goal and success metrics to meet
with you. We dig deep into your
business and audiences to
understand the challenges and its
customers

05. Strategy

Before starting to design & develop
we share our ideas, wireframes or
rough sketches with you and see if
we are on the right track to avoid
waste of time and efforts.

06. Design

After establishing a strong strategic
foundation for the project we move
on to try and share with you the
visual concepts and go for the
agreed solution

07. Implementation

We start developing the website
using your selection of technology. If
you are not sure of the platform we
will guide you through the best
systems out there

08. Feedback

We are not done when the project is
done we track the performance and
the status of the website by
checking on you regularly and take
action when required.
